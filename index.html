Yang Xiao

I am a second year PhD student at Imagine, where I work on computer vision and deep learning.

My research interests are centered around Deep Learning, Computer Vision and Object Pose Estimation.

My advisors are Renaud MARLET, and Mathieu AUBRY. I've spent time at Mines ParisTech Centre for Mathematical Morphology, and at CentraleSupelec L2S Telecoms and Networks group.

I received my master degree in Signal and Image Processing from University Paris-Sud and my bachelor degree in Optical and Electronic Information from Huazhong University of Science and Technology.
